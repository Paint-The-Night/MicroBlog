Villans Land In Magic Kingdom Will Destory a Perfect Design

Published: 2024.09.12

Blog Entry: 4

Introduction

I started writing this post as a quick reaction to all the D23 2024 annoucments and started with Villans Land. The longer I wrote the more I realised this would have to be an fuller exploration on layout design of Disneyland / Magic Kingdom and why I think Disney is making a giant mistake with this decision. So while this article has a negative spin from the new annoucment I really want to highlight the absolute work of art that is the original land layout and design.

The Classic Design

Before I explain why the Villans Land annoucment is such a terrible idea I need to paint a picture of the currnet state of The Magic Kingdom and the classic hub-and-spoke design of Disneyland.

Of all the parks Disney owns and operate around the world they're split into roughly 3 different styles of parks (I'm not including Water Parks here)

Castle Parks

  • Disneyland California
  • Magic Kingdom
  • Disneyland Paris
  • Tokyo Disneyland
  • Hong Kong Disneyland
  • Shaghai Disneyland

Studio Parks

  • Disneyland California Adventure
  • Disney Hollywood Studios
  • Disney Studios Paris

Other

  • Disney Animal Kingdom
  • Disney Epcot
  • Disney Sea Tokyo

So I'll be refering to the castle parks in general (as they all follow a very similar design) from this point on

The formular of a castle park is near perfect. A narrow walkway down classic americana towards the castle centrepeice. To the left a land of adventure and yesteryear, straight ahead through the castle a world of fantasy and right will take you into a world of tomorrow. I think Walt Disney himself instilled the essance of Disneyland's feel in his opening speech

Here age relives fond memories of the past -- and here youth may savor the challenge and promise of the future. Disneyland is dedicated to the ideals, the dreams and the hard facts that have created America -- with the hope that it will be a source of joy and inspiration to all the world. - Walt Disney

Villains Land

A few years ago I might have been very enthusiatic about this annoucument. I still think the core idea is good and they could create an amazing land around this idea. I love disney villans and I'm happy they're creating an area that's not based around a single IP. If done right the can bring back some classic imagineering that feels aligned with some of the most iconic lands. However I'm 100% againts Villans Land being in a Castle Park.
